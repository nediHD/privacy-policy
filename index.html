Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Njemački kroz priče ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to our web application available at njemackikrozprice.com.

Age Requirement

Our service is intended for users aged 16 or older in accordance with EU GDPR requirements. By registering, you confirm that you meet this requirement.

Data We Collect

  • Email address (for account creation and login)
  • First and last name (provided during registration)
  • Learning progress (stories read, words learned, daily streak)
  • Subscription status and payment information (processed by Stripe)
  • Feedback messages and optional screenshots (submitted voluntarily)
  • Error reports and technical data (via Sentry)
  • Session data stored in browser localStorage (required for login)

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the app
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To track your learning progress (streak, words, stories)
  • To fix bugs and crashes
  • To respond to feedback you submit

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase – database, authentication and file storage (EU region)
  • Stripe – payment processing (PCI compliant, Stripe never shares card data with us)
  • Sentry – error tracking (collects browser info, IP address, stack traces)

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store your session token (required for login) and privacy consent. Stripe uses its own cookies on the payment page for fraud detection. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the app (Profile → Delete account), which permanently removes all your personal data.

Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to delete your data (available directly in the app)
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent

Contact

For any privacy-related requests: njemackikrozprice@gmail.com
